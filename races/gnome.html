GNOME

"Slight, sly, fey tricksters who excell at avoiding notice."

Racial Traits

Darkvision: Gnomes can see up to 60 ft away in total darkness.

Small: Gnomes have a +2 bonus to AC against opportunity attacks, to disengage checks, and to skill checks involving stealth.

Confounding (Racial Power)

Once per battle, when you roll a natural 16+ with an attack, you can also daze the target until the end of your turn. When the escalation die reaches 3, you gain an additional use of Confounding.

Adventurer Feat: You may instead weaken the target until the end of your next turn. In addition, when the escalation die reaches 5+, you may trigger Confounding on any hit.
