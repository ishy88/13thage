ORC

"Fierce warriors imbued with primal savagery."

Racial Traits

Low-Light Vision: You do not suffer penalties due to dim light.

Orcish Visage: You gain a +2 bonus to skill checks involving being intimidating or threatening..

Lethal (Racial Power)

Once per battle, reroll a melee attack roll and use the roll you prefer as a result.

Adventurer Feat: When the escalation die turns to 3, you gain one additional use of Lethal.
