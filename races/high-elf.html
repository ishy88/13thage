HIGH ELF

"Graceful warriors and wizards at home in the eldritch twilight of the deep forests."

Racial Traits

Low-Light Vision: You do not suffer penalties due to dim light.

Elven Awareness: You gain a +2 bonus to skill checks involving perception and sensing magic.

Highblood Teleport (Racial Power)

Once per battle, as a move action, place yourself in a nearby location you can see. When the escalation die turns to an odd number, you regain the use of Highblood Teleport.

Adventurer Feat: Deal damage equal to twice your level to one enemy engaged with you before or after you teleport.

Champion Feat: When you teleport, you gain a +2 bonus to your defenses until the start of your next turn.
