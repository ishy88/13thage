LIZARDFOLK

"Proud, honorable warriors, born from the blood of an ancient snake-headed god."

Racial Traits

Low-Light Vision: You do not suffer penalties due to dim light.

Claws and Jaws: You do not take a -2 penalty to your unarmed attacks. In addition, your unarmed attacks deal 1d6 damage per level, not 1d6 damage per ever other level.

Taste for Blood (Racial Power)

Once per battle, deal ongoing damage to a target you hit with a natural even attack roll as a free action. The ongoing damage equals 5 times your level. A normal save (11+) ends the damage.

Adventurer Feat: When the escalation die reaches 3, you gain one additional use of Taste for Blood.

Champion Feat: The target must succeed on a hard save (16+) to end the ongoing damage.
