HALF ELF

"Born heroes and leaders who combine the best features of humans and elves."

Racial Traits

Low-Light Vision: You do not suffer penalties due to dim light.

Elven Awareness: You gain a +2 bonus to skill checks involving perception and sensing magic.

Surprising Result (Racial Power)

Once per battle, subtract or add one to a natural result of one of your own d20 rolls. When the escalation die reaches 3, you gain an additional use of Surprising Result.

Adventurer Feat: You may instead subtract or add two from a natural result.
