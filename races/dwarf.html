DWARF

"Masters of stone and iron, dauntless and unyielding in the face of adversity."

Racial Traits

Darkvision: Dwarves can see up to 60 ft away in total darkness.

Stonecunning: Dwarves gain a +2 bonus to skill checks involving architecture or stonework.

Is That Your Best Shot? (Racial Power)

Once per battle as a free action after you have been hit by an enemy attack, you can heal using a recovery. If the escalation die is less than 2, you only get half the usual healing from the recovery. Note that you can’t use this ability if the attack drops you to 0 hp or below. You’ve got to be on your feet to sneer at their attack and recover.

Champion Feat: If the escalation die is 2+ when you use this power, the recovery is free.
