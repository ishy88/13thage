HALFLING

"Quick and resourceful wanderers, small in stature but great in courage."

Racial Traits

Small: Gnomes have a +2 bonus to AC against opportunity attacks, to disengage checks, and to skill checks involving stealth.

Evasive (Racial Power)

Once per battle, when an enemy would hit you, you may add the escalation die to your AC and PD against that attack.

Adventurer Feat: Until the end of your next turn, you also gain a bonus to attack against that enemy equal to the escalation die.

Champion Feat: When the escalation die reaches 3, you gain an additional use of Evasive
